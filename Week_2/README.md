# 📊 E-Commerce Sales Analysis Dashboard

## Project Overview

This project was completed as part of the DecodeLabs Data Analytics Internship Program.

The project focuses on performing Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) and building an interactive dashboard using transactional e-commerce data to extract meaningful business insights and support data-driven decision making.

---

## Objectives

The main objectives of this project include:

* Analyze overall sales performance
* Identify top-performing products
* Understand customer purchasing behavior
* Evaluate marketing channel effectiveness
* Detect sales trends over time
* Build an interactive dashboard for business reporting

---

## Dataset Information

The dataset contains transactional sales and order information including:

* Order ID
* Order Date
* Customer Information
* Product Category
* Quantity
* Total Price
* Payment Method
* Order Status
* Referral Source

**Dataset Size:** 1200 Orders

---

## Data Cleaning Process

The following cleaning procedures were performed before analysis:

* Handled missing values in CouponCode
* Standardized categorical values
* Extracted Month and Year from Order Date
* Verified duplicates and inconsistencies
* Validated numerical fields

## Key Insights

* Instagram generates the highest customer acquisition performance
* Online payments represent the most commonly used payment method
* Products such as Printers, Laptops, and Chairs generate significant revenue
* Revenue fluctuates across months with noticeable peak periods
* Most customer orders are successfully delivered

---

## Business Recommendations

* Increase investment in high-performing marketing channels
* Focus promotional campaigns on high-revenue products
* Encourage digital payment adoption through incentives
* Investigate causes of cancelled orders
* Optimize marketing during lower-performing periods
